Kasey is a native of Colorado born and raised in Colorado Springs. She has a Bachelor of Visual Communications that she earned at Collins College in Tempe Arizona in 1999. She specializes in Graphic Arts, Illustration, Website Design and Marketing. Kasey has over 20 years of experience in a vast array of design applications, from printed marketing materials to large scale mural paintings. Her specialty over the years has been graphic arts and art direction for many companies that she has worked for and also as a contracted freelancer with her business KLR Designs. Browse her portfolio collection to see many samples of her work. With a natural talent and eye for design Kasey is an invaluable asset to any company. She is adding to her arsenal of skills a full stack web development certificate that is currently being earned from Denver University. All websites, pictures, illustrations and paintings on this site are designed/drawn/painted by Kasey.

In her spare time she is a very active person who truly enjoys the outdoors. Being an avid skier, snowboarder, trail runner, mountain biker, hiker and so much more in the great state of Colorado.
